About

Psionic Labs are used for training the Psi Operative class. Upon construction, there is one slot to train Psi Operatives, but the facility can be upgraded to include an additional slot, allowing Psi Operatives to be trained in parallel. There are also Scientist slots that increase the rate at which Psi Operatives learn their class or their new ability.

Unlike in the base game, Psi Operatives need experience in the field to become eligible for more training. See the Psi Operative page for more details.
