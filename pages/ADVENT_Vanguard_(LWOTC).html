The Advent Vanguard is an elite trooper variant specializing in close combat similar to XCOM's Assaults .

The Vanguard is one of ADVENT's most elite troopers and one of the most dangerous troopers they can field. These troopers specialize in close combat gaining more crit as they enter point blank range on top of the biggest threat, Close Combat Specialist , granting them a reaction shot within 4 tiles against targets that act. Vanguards pursue to break through the enemy front lines and cause havoc. Unlike most units in this game, they can inflict rupture. permanently increasing damage taken for the rest of the mission making them dangerous on their own but also making them a large threat along with groups of enemies.

Tips

  • Like most flankable units, they will ideally try to find reasonable cover whenever flanked. This can be taken advantage of if you want to force enemies to move. Although Vanguards do aim for cover still, they will continuously pursue you in order to enter close range so this may not be that effective.
  • Close Combat Specialist makes them incredibly dangerous in close range on top of their other bonuses granting them a reaction shot when acting within 4 tiles. To avoid these shots it is best to use moves that disables abilities or reaction attacks such as disorienting them or suppressing them.
  • Unlike most units in the game, they have one of the few sources of rupture which will permanently increase damage taken from attacks for the mission. As if they weren't already a great menace with great damage and disruptiveness, they should be a priority target in order to prevent rupture from being inflicted onto your team.
