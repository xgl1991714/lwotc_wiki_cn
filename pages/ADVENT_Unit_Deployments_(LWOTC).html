In each mission's tactical layer, ADVENT will deploy a certain amount of enemies present from the start. The deployed enemies are grouped together in pods of up to 8 units, consisting of a Leader and their Followers. The type of units that can appear in a pod is dependant on the schedule of the mission, which is chosen randomly from a few options depending on ADVENT's Strength Level in the region, the mission's innate modifier, and the Infiltration Value of the squad at the moment of carrying out the mission. Once the specific pods are determined a Leader is chosen from specific inclusion/exclusion lists and the spawn weights according to ADVENT's Force Level (some pods may also modify this level), and appropriate followers for that Leader are chosen afterwards up to the pod's limit.

ADVENT's Force Level will advance periodically after a number of days (dependent on the game's difficulty level) and it can also be modified for certain missions, and as it progresses obsolete units will be removed and more advanced ones will be added for possible deployment. Force Levels start at 1 and can reach up to 20, any Force Level beyond will not affect enemy composition.

ADVENT's Strength Level is more variable and can be affected significatively more by XCOM's actions, such as attacking ADVENT's reinforcements between regions, or attracting ADVENT's attention in other regions so they're forced to divert Strength from the target region. Minimum Strength Level is 1, and it can rise up indefinitely, depending on how many schedules are defined for the mission.

Unit Spawn Weights

Each unit has a specific weight for each Force Level, indicating its probability of being chosen for an enemy pod. The Alien to ADVENT weights are not equivalent, except in the Regional Network Tower, Central Network Tower and Alien Fortress missions. The weight for an Alien Leader to be picked is reduced to 67%, and for Alien led groups, the weight for Alien Followers is reduced to 50%.

Leaders have increased Force Level depending on difficulty (assumed 0, 1, 2 and 3 for Rookie, Veteran, Commander and Legend).

Notes

  • Some units can only appear either as Leaders or Followers.
  • LW2 will modify some generated pods depending on various circumstances:
    • DLC and Alien Fortress missions will not be modified at all.
    • Pods with PROTECTED, LIST_BOSSx[X]_Standard and LIST_BOSSx[X]_Extra or Chryssalids in the name will not be modified.
    • Codexes and Avatars will not appear if their first story-related appearances haven't been found yet. Codex Leaders at any point of the campaign will also be forcefully switched out, save for lone Codexes or otherwise unmodifiable pods.
    • Forced reinforcement ADVENT Officers that appear in vanilla while the Skulljack objectives are active will be rerolled.
    • If there's more than one of the same Alien in an Alien led pod the pod will be diversified.
    • If there's more than the Max per Group units in a pod the pod will be diversified.
    • If the pod consists of 4-5 units and there's more than the pod size - 1 of the same unit the pod will be diversified.
    • If the pod consists of 6+ units and there's more than the pod size - 2 of the same unit the pod will be diversified.
  • Turrets spawn by a different mechanic, up to the max number of turrets. Both turret versions are identical in stats.
  • Generals are not randomly generated, they will only appear predeterminated in boss pods.
  • If there are no possible characters to fill a pod with, it will be filled with ADVENT Troopers.
