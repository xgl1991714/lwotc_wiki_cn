当你研究了反抗军通信、联系了某个大洲的所有区域，并且在该大洲建造或控制了所需数量的信号塔时，将获得大洲奖励：对于2区域的大洲，需要1个信号塔；对于3或4区域的大洲，则需要2个信号塔。

奖励列表

  • Ballistics Modelling
    • The speed of all weapons research is increased by 15%.
  • 隐藏储备II
    • “复仇者号”获得额外的5点电力。
  • Inside Job II
    • All Intel rewards increased by 15%.
  • Inside Knowledge
    • The effect of all weapon modifications is increased.
  • Mental Fortitude
    • All battle madness (panic, obsession, berserk, shattered) only lasts one turn.
  • Noble Cause
    • Will recovery in all soldiers is 30% faster.
  • Popular Support II
    • Supplies collected from each Supply Drop are increased by 20%.
  • Pursuit of Knowledge
    • Laboratory facilities provide an additional 10% boost to research times.
  • Quid Pro Quo
    • All Black Market costs reduced by 20%.
  • Rapid Collection
    • Resistance supply drops are collected instantly.
  • Recruiting Centers
    • New recruits cost 10 less Supplies.
  • Resistance Network
    • Contact with new regions is made instantly.
  • Under the Table II
    • The Black Market pays a 20% Supply premium for goods.
