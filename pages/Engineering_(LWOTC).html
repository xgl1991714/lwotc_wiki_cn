Engineering in LWOTC is mostly similar to vanilla. Weapons, armor, and items are built using supplies, alloys, elerium, and elerium cores. Unlike vanilla, all items are manufactured on an individual basis rather than as squad-wide upgrades. Notably, all of the vests and ammo types have been moved from the Proving Ground to engineering once the projects have been completed. All items are produced instantly.
