This is a page that lists helpful resources and guides that may assist you in creating a strategy or gain tips on how to beat LWOTC or to gain a better understanding of the various new mechanics


Commander's Journal - Articles made by Casey that goes over their experience in beating LWOTC and various tips for the game.

Hit Chance Calculator and Visualizer - A hit chance calculator and visualizer by Copyrite that demonstrates how the final hit chance results are calculated based on the final hit chance, crit chance, dodge chance and graze band value.

Mission Table and Detection Calculator - This is a helpful spreadsheet that contains the missions within LWOTC and various details about them as well as containing a calculator that calculates the chance to detect a mission with a given setup.

DerAva LWOTC Guides/Tutorials - This is a playlist by DerAva that goes over the new LWOTC strategy layer mechanics in-depth.

LWOTC Written Guide - This is a written guide by inukealot that goes over the new LWOTC mechanics and changes from the base game.
