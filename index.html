Welcome to the XCOM 2: Long War of the Chosen wiki, a part of UFOPaedia.org . Long War of the Chosen (LWOTC) is a mod primarily focused on porting Long War 2 to work with the XCOM 2: War of the Chosen expansion, but includes several important changes from Long War 2 as well. As Long War 2 did, Long War of the Chosen introduces many changes, large and small, to the vanilla version of the game. It is longer and more complex than the vanilla version but gives you many more tools to combat the alien threat.

The main channel to discuss the mod with its creators and community is the Long War of the Chosen Discord server .

If you're looking for the original mod for XCOM 2 , see Long War 2 . If you're looking for the original mod for XCOM:EU/EW , see Long War .

Contributing

Like all wikis, we depend on individual contributors to keep information up to date. You're welcome to contribute to this wiki by creating a UFOpaedia account and editing the pages. While editing, pay particular attention to changes LWOTC makes compared to LW2 and WotC. Please use the Talk button to discuss any aspects of this wiki. The version against which this wiki should be kept up to date is the main release on Steam Workshop .
